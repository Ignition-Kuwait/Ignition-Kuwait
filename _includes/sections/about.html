About Ignition!

Ignition was founded by a group of young Kuwaities from various disciplines: Civil, Architectural, Electrical, and Computer. What brought these four together is a shared desire to take Kuwait to space through research, development, and education. To realize this goal, the four founders launched Ignition. Ignition is the Kuwaiti branch of the Mars Society, and operates as a nonprofit youth-led organization. The purpose of Ignition is to lay the groundwork for the first Kuwaiti space society which will play a key role in the Kuwaiti scientific research field.
