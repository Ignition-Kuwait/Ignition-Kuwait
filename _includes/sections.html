About Ignition!

Ignition was founded by a group of young Kuwaities from various disciplines: Civil, Architectural, Electrical, and Computer. What brought these four together is a shared desire to take Kuwait to space through research, development, and education. To realize this goal, the four founders launched Ignition. Ignition is the Kuwaiti branch of the Mars Society, and operates as a nonprofit youth-led organization. The purpose of Ignition is to lay the groundwork for the first Kuwaiti space society which will play a key role in the Kuwaiti scientific research field. Ignition’s vision is to support the growth of society in terms of research, education, leadership, community engagement, and innovation.

Core Principals

Mission

Ignition’s mission is to create Kuwait’s first ever space exploration program and have a direct contribution to scientific development.

Vision

Ignition’s vision is to advance science and human civilization while spreading peace and encouraging global cooperation.

Values

Ignition’s vision is to instill the belief and faith in youth leadership, to accomplish the endeavor of Space.

As we contribute to space projects, experiments and missions, we are creating more and more awareness around space. We seek to include different professional and academic backgrounds in all our endeavours, because we believe that the space industry is a place for all. By creating the opportunities for ourselves and future generations, we will increase our presence in space.
